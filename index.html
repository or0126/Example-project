About HYDRA Boat Club

HYDRA B.C's members represent a diverse mix of all ages and skill levels. While many who rowed since youth and are returning to the sport as masters, others have only recently learned to row through one of our Learn-to-Row or Learn-to-Scull activities.

HYDRA B.C owns a mix of boats of all sizes (8+, 4+, 4x, 2x, 1x,) and types (coastal, wide etc..). These boats are used at HYDRA B.C practices and are available for the Club Rowers Activities. In addition, many individual members pay an additional annual fee to store their private boat at our facilities.

HYDRA B.C prides itself on providing a forum to meet the needs of all of its members. Many of our members do not race but participate regularly in Club Row practices for general exercise and as a great way to socialize and get fit. Many others, however, compete regularly in regattas around the region in one of the HYDRA B.C “team boats” or as independent rowers in smaller boats like singles and doubles. HYDRA B.C’s Managers coordinate the racing calendar and help members to make arrangements for getting their boat to regattas, registering for races, etc.
